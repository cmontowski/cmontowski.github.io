Winter Walks

While it may be quite

While the air is still

Take a winter walk with me

Breath the frosty air

See the falling snow

Feel the crunching beneath your feet

Bundle up your fingertips

And cover up your nose

Winter Hiking

It might be cold but the snow can be it's own kind of beauty. It can involve trees covered in their sparkling coats or the lakes and rivers filled with ice. There is a certain kind of stillness that only winter brings on.

Sometimes we don't want to go outside in winter because it's cold, or because there's snow but this can just be an opportunity for different types of hiking. Maybe you just need snowshoes to make it happen
