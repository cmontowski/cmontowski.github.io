Spring Hiking

New life is coming. Everything is beginning to grow and nature is once again teeming with life. This is an excellent opportunity to get outside and spend some time exploring. Anything from a stroll along a bubbling stream filled with the icey runoff to a hike along a mountain ridge.

Sometimes we get a little cooped up by the end of the winter and the fresh air can also remind us of fresh opportunities for the new year.
