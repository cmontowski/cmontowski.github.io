Summer Hiking

Summer hiking can be some of the most enjoyable. The long days give you an opportunity to go for longer hikes and backpacking is often easiest this time of year.Everything is green and aive and the best part is if you get to hot you can just go swimming.

Sometimes summer can give us an opportunity to slow down and connect with our roots. From backpacking to walking on the beach getting outside can give us a better perspective on things
