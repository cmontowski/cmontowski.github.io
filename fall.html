Fall Hiking

Even though we often feel like fall is a time to refocus and dive back in to work the autumn colors and milder temperatures should encourage us to keep going outside and experiencing nature.

Sometimes we need to go outside even while we are trying to refocus into school and work. Fall walks can give us an opportunity to reflect and experience nature preparing to take a long winter nap.
